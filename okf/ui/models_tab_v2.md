This concept documents the Sage Utils Models Tab V2, focusing on model browsing, selection, and fast actions.

## Key capabilities

- **Browser-style model list** instead of dropdowns.
- **Folder hierarchy** with collapsible folders and counts.
- **Keyboard navigation** support for arrow keys, Enter, Home, End, Page Up/Down.
- **Filtering and sorting** by model type, usage, and name.
- **Hover actions** for copy, details, and quick operations.

## Recommended workflow

1. Open the Models Tab.
2. Browse models in the folder hierarchy.
3. Use the filter box to narrow results.
4. Select a model with click or keyboard navigation.
5. Use hover buttons for quick actions like copy path or select.

## Benefits

- Easier management of large model collections.
- Faster selection and discovery of checkpoints, LoRAs, and ControlNets.
- Better keyboard-first usability for power users.
